Preface

Accompanying with tsunami, landslide and other secondary disasters, earthquakes, from "Hanshin-Awaji-daishinsai" to "Wenchuang Earthquake", have led to uncountable deaths and injuries, causing a loss of trillions of dollars. Inspired by "Where Do Birth Generations Live" and "Where Are The Jobs", I will explore the data of the earthquakes in the US in spatial thinking and visualize my work done with the assistance of Leaflet.js.

The Data Source

The data employed in this work stems from USGS Earthquake Hazard Program. The earthquake data covers the Conterminous US including 48 states(Hawaii and Alaska are excluded). Otherwise, OpenStreetMap is used as the base map.
